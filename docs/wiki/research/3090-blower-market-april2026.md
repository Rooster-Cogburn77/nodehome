## Confirmed Does NOT Exist
- **PNY RTX 3090 Blower** - All PNY 3090s are XLR8 open-air. No blower variant was ever produced.
- **Dell OEM RTX 3090 Blower** - Dell OEM 3090s are dual-fan, NOT blower style.
- **NVIDIA Founders Edition as blower** - FE is a flow-through cooler design, 2.5+ slots, not a true single-fan rear-exhaust blower.

# GPU Resale Side Hustle Analysis

**Status:** Explored, not committed
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-04

## Business Model
Blower 3090 arbitrage: ITAD vendors don't price the blower premium, but retail AI builders pay 10-25% more for blower form factor.

## Acquisition
- **ITAD cost:** $500-650/card (bulk, untested)
- **eBay sold prices:** Trending up 37% over 5 months
- **Blower premium:** 10-25% over open-air equivalents

## Tiered Model (Proposed)
1. Individual cards (simplest, lowest margin)
2. Pre-configured kits (RAM + GPU bundles)
3. Assembled servers (highest margin, most work)

## Kill Shots Identified
1. **Authenticity/testing** - Need to verify cards work before resale
2. **Buy price** - ITAD acquisition cost determines viability
3. **Volume** - Need consistent supply, not one-offs
4. **Returns/warranty** - Need to handle DOA cards

## Margin Math
- Buy at $500-650, sell at $1,000-1,100
- Gross margin: $350-600/card
- After eBay fees (~13%), shipping, testing time: $200-400 net/card
- Need 5-10 cards/month to be meaningful
